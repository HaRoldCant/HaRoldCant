## 🧠 Why Sociology + Cybersecurity?

Most security breaches don't happen because of technical failures — they happen because of **human behavior**. Phishing, social engineering, insider threats, and poor security culture are all deeply human problems.

My background helps me think about:
- 🎭 **Social engineering** — why people trust, obey, and make risky decisions
- 📋 **Security policies** — writing rules that humans actually follow
- 🔍 **Threat profiling** — understanding the motivations of attackers and victims
